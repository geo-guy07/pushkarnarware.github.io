Who I Am

I'm a first-year B.Tech CSE student at MITS Gwalior, building toward a future at the intersection of computational geoscience and data-driven problem-solving. My long-term goal is a Master's in Earth Systems or Environmental Data Science — and eventually, working on real geo-data problems like flood modeling, climate risk, and water scarcity.

My path isn't typical for a CS student. I chose this field not because of software jobs, but because Earth systems need programmers — people who can process satellite imagery, build climate dashboards, and make sense of messy environmental datasets. I'm working toward that profile one project at a time.

Right now I'm deepening my foundation in Python, data science, and systems programming while building projects that reflect my actual interests — not just resume filler. I believe the most interesting work happens at the edge where disciplines collide.
